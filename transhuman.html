Transhuman

Published: Wed 01 November 2000
By Sifting

In Projects.

I love a good MUD, in particular Dragon Realms. The depth and potential for immersive experiences in these games cannot be overstated. So it was, more or less on a whim, that I ended up playing around with the idea of writing my own. While there is a glut of high fantasy MUDs, there are, to my knowledge no science fiction ones.

The game runs in a browser and uses websockets. The server was written in D, and as there were no websocket hosts for D at the that time, or indeed, at all, I had to write my own implementation! The game logic itself is all done in Lua.

We found right away in test playing that this little project has something special. All players start with a randomly generated character, and must struggle to establish and maintain themselves in a ruthless byzantine dystopia. I look forward to sharing this one!
