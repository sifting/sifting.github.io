Traum

Published: Sat 01 July 2000
By Sifting

In Projects.

Back in late 2019 I was talking with a friend and we got the idea to put together a little demo for the Sega Dreamcast. A month later, Traum came into the world. See for yourself below!

Time lapse

Exploration

Sunrise

I wrote Traum from the ground up to be cross platform. It compiles and runs under the Dreamcast, Linux and Windows. Developing for the Dreamcast was surprisingly modern, thanks to a fairly active community that supports a completely open toolchain and kernel for the 20 year old system! Testing and debugging was done via ethernet, much in the same way one might use a USB cable for Android or iOS work today.

The level geometry and entity placement were done in Blender, then exported into an immediate format, which was then parsed by a tool that I wrote to process it all into something the engine could use. The sky and the celestial bodies are procedural effect.

Since then, we have begun work on Traum II, which we hope to show off soon!
